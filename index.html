Welcome to my website. I am using it to post infographics, lists, maps, and other content I created.

In the past I have created alternative history maps, world maps of the present, family trees on various topics such as languages or religions, family trees of operating systems and other software, and software comparison tables. The topics of my graphics include the humanities (lingustics, history, politics etc.) and also, as a completely unrelated area, software and technology. Some of my graphics have been posted on the web already, while some others leave my harddisk for the very first time.

Previously you could find this website at Blogspot and Neocities.
